# Passions

Beyond my professional work, I remain deeply interested in environmental systems and in how landscapes evolve under pressure from climate variability and human activity.

I also care about how people learn—both in academic environments and beyond. This connects to a broader curiosity about education, personal development, and the role of reflection in professional life.

As a parent, I keep learning about leadership and guidance in a very practical way. It is a constant reminder that learning is rarely linear, and that growth often happens through exploration, dialogue, and patience.
