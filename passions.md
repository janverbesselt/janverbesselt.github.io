# Passions

Beyond my professional work, I remain deeply interested in environmental systems, fieldwork, and understanding how landscapes evolve under pressure from climate and human activity.

I am also interested in how people learn — both in academic environments and beyond. This connects to my broader curiosity about education, personal development, and the role of reflection in professional life.

As a parent, I keep learning about leadership and guidance in a very practical way. It is a constant reminder that learning is rarely linear, and that growth often happens through exploration, dialogue, and patience.
