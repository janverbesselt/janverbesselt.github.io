## Key Contributions

One of my core contributions is the development of [BFAST](https://github.com/bfast2/bfast) (Breaks For Additive Seasonal and Trend) — an open-source approach for detecting and characterising change in satellite image time series. This project has supported research and operational applications worldwide, and served as a platform for international collaboration and capacity building.

See also the [BFAST tutorials](https://janverbesselt.github.io/BFASTforAEO/) for applied examples.
