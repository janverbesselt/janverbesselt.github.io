## Selected contributions

A core contribution is the development of **BFAST** (Breaks For Additive Seasonal and Trend), an open-source approach for detecting and characterising change in time series. The project has supported research and operational applications and has served as a platform for international collaboration.

## Projects

My work has involved competitive funding and collaborations across ESA, European programmes, and international partners. Selected examples include:

- OpenEO (EU Horizon 2020): enabling scalable EO analytics
- SEPAL (FAO): implementation and capacity building for time series monitoring
- Copernicus Global Land monitoring contributions
- Research and teaching materials for large-scale EO processing and analysis
